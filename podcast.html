The Aspiring STEM Geek

The Aspiring STEM Geek is a podcast I created to have a casual conversation with people that work in or are interested in Science, Math, Engineering and Technology fields. The experience levels range from early career to decades of experience and the topics of discussion range from deep dives into data centers and chile peppers to what video games the guests are playing or what books they are reading. Hopefully there is an episode in the archive that interests you!
